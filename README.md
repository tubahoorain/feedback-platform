<h1>FeedbackSaaS - Multi-Tenant Customer Feedback Platform</h1>

<p>
A modern, multi-tenant SaaS platform that allows businesses to collect, analyze, and manage customer feedback efficiently. 
Featuring a dual-module system <strong>(Star Ratings & Emoji Reactions)</strong> with a stunning <strong>Glassmorphism UI</strong>.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>‚≠ê Key Features</h2>

<h3>For Business Owners (Admin)</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Real-time Dashboard:</strong> View total feedbacks, average rating, and live sentiment analysis.</li>

  <li><strong>Dual Feedback Modules:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>‚≠ê Star Rating (1-5)</li>
      <li>üòÄ Emoji Reactions</li>
    </ul>
  </li>

  <li><strong>Full Customization:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Change Brand Name & Colors (Live Preview)</li>
      <li>Upload Company Logo</li>
      <li>Create dynamic feedback tags (e.g., "Food", "Service")</li>
    </ul>
  </li>

  <li><strong>Smart Logic:</strong> Tags auto-change based on rating 
    (e.g., Low rating ‚Üí "What went wrong?", High rating ‚Üí "What did you like?").</li>

  <li><strong>Security:</strong> Secure Login/Logout and Data Reset functionality.</li>
</ul>

<h3>For Customers (Feedback Users)</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Immersive UI:</strong> Beautiful 3D background with floating spheres and Glassmorphism effects.</li>
  <li><strong>Mobile-First Design:</strong> Optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops.</li>
  <li><strong>Instant Feedback:</strong> No login required for customers.</li>
</ul>

<h3>üõ°Ô∏è For Super Admin</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Tenant Management:</strong> Approve or Reject new company registrations.</li>
  <li><strong>Revenue Tracking:</strong> View total registered companies and estimated revenue.</li>
  <li><strong>Payment Verification:</strong> Verify Easypaisa/JazzCash/Bank transactions before activating accounts.</li>
</ul>
